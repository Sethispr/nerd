Nerd Documentation

1Introduction

Nerd Bot is a specialized Discord bot designed for calculating your stats. This guide will help you understand how to use the bot effectively.

2Basic Usage

The primary command for the bot is /calc. This command processes your in-game statistics and returns detailed metrics.

Command Format:
/calc [experience gained] [rounds played] [targets eliminated] [escapes] [targets protected] [damage dealt] [final shots] [ffa kills] [ffa wins] [infected killed] [infection survival] [infections] [epidemic] [sdi] [device]

3Parameters Explained

  • Experience: XPB minus XPA value
  • SDI: Server Difficulty Index (typically between 0.9 and 1.3)
  • Device: Your main playing platform (pc, phone, tablet, console)

4Example Usage

/calc 92086 221 3 3 72 120133 45 25 441 16 321 14 30 3 0.9992 pc
[Note]: Type in /calc then press the "Enter" key and finally paste in your stats similar to the format above.

Metrics Explained

  • OP (Overall Performance): Your total performance score combining all stats
  • TP (Tactical Performance): Score based on your tactical abilities
  • SP (Special Performance): Score reflecting your performance in special game modes (ffa, infection)
  • SDI (Server Difficulty Index): Reflects the server's difficulty level
